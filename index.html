    MOJITO

    Ingredients

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1/2 lime, cut into 4 wedges
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces white rum
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup club soda

    • How to prepare

  • Place mint leaves and 1 lime wedge into a sturdy glass.
  • Use a muddler to crush the mint and lime to release the mint oils and lime juice.
  • Add 2 more lime wedges and the sugar, and muddle again to release the lime juice.
  • Do not strain the mixture.
  • Fill the glass almost to the top with ice. Pour the rum over the ice, and fill the glass with carbonated water.
  • Stir, taste, and add more sugar if desired. Garnish with the remaining lime wedge.

Mojito

Medium

A classic cocktail originating from cuba. Lets you feel the cold breeze on a warm summer evening. Mojito will tingle your tongue with its sugary taste, enliven you with its gentle touch of alcohol and cool you off with its minty aftertaste.

    Bloody Mary

    Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces vodka
  • 3 ounces tomato juice
  • 1/2 ounce lemon juice
  • 1 dash of Worcestershire sauce

    Rainbow shots

    Ingredients

  • 6oz Pineapple juice
  • 1/2oz grenadine
  • 1oz vodka
  • 3/4oz blue curaçao
  • crushed ice

    • How to prepare

  • Measure out 1/2oz. of grenadine and carefully pour it into the bottom of the shaker, being careful not to get it on the sides.
  • Gently add crushed ice to the shaker until it is 3/4 full.
  • Place a big spoon over the ice and slowly pour 6oz. of pineapple juice into the shaker.
  • Leave the big spoon resting atop the ice and pour 1oz. of vodka slowly onto the spoon.
  • Slowly add 3/4oz. blue curaçao to the tip of the spoon only.
  • Start pouring the shots right away from the side of the shaker your added the blue curaçao to.

Rainbow shots

These are one of the best party shots ever. There's nothing like a good magic show and Rainbow Shots definitely deliver - when you pour many different colored shots out of the same shaker right in front of your guests eyes?!! A perfect presto.

    Irish Coffee

    Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz Parts Irish Whiskey
  • 3 oz Parts Hot Coffee
  • 1 oz Heavy Cream (Slightly Whipped)
  • 1 Tsp Brown Sugar

    • How to prepare

  • Pour hot water into serving glass to heat.
  • Toss hot water, pour in coffee and stir in sugar until dissolved.
  • Add whiskey, stir to combine and float cream on top. Enjoy responsibly!
